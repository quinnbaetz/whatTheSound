What The Sound!

No words. Just sounds. Go!

How to Play
  1. One player is the Sounder — they see the word on screen.
  2. The Sounder must get others to guess using sounds only — no words, no humming song lyrics, no mouthing, no pointing.
  3. Press Next ✓ when the team guesses correctly (earns a point).
  4. Press Skip ✗ to pass on a word (no penalty).
  5. You have 60 seconds per turn. Go!
  6. Words get harder as rounds go on. Good luck. 🔊
